I'm heidi! I use they/she pronouns and identify as non-binary. I was born in West England and my study and practice continues here, based primarily in Bristol. My practice began in theatre as a writer, director and performer and has now moved to immersive arts, games, and play in all forms. I am most interested in working with queer methodologies: embrace glitching the system, new expressions of bodies, and an appreciation for the animal.
